MM..Tiles

a creation by Will McAllister

drawing inspiration from MF Doom cover art, drawn by artist Jason Jagel, I created MM..Tiles, a stylized vector tile basemap using Mapbox Studio.

Check out Jagel's work

I evoked a cartoony style by varying fonts across the map.

Colors across the map are drawn from the two album covers, MM..Food and Doomsday
